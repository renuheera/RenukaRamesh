Ven Pongal

Comfort food of Tamil Nadu — creamy, peppery, and satisfying

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (yellow)
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 10 cashews, curry leaves, ghee, salt

Instructions

  1. Dry roast moong dal until aromatic. Wash rice and dal together.
  2. Pressure cook with 4 cups water and salt until soft.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan, fry cashews, then add cumin, pepper, ginger, curry leaves.
  4. Pour this tempering over the cooked pongal and mix well.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar!
