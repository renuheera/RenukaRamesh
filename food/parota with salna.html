Parotta with Salna

Flaky layers meet spicy, rich salna — street food royalty!

Ingredients

  • Parotta (store-bought or homemade) - 4 pieces
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds, 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder
  • Curry leaves, salt to taste, oil
  • Optional: boiled eggs or chicken pieces for extra yum

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add fennel seeds and curry leaves.
  2. Sauté onions and green chilies until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook till oil separates.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and simmer on low heat for 10 minutes.
  6. Optional: Add boiled eggs or chicken pieces and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with flaky parottas and soak up the spicy salna goodness!
