Idli & Sambar

Soft, fluffy and full of homely goodness!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Sambar with toor dal, veggies, and tamarind

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and dal separately, grind and ferment overnight.
  2. Pour into idli moulds and steam for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare sambar with your favorite veggies.
  4. Serve hot idli with piping sambar & chutney.
