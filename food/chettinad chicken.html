Chettinad Chicken

Fiery, aromatic, and irresistibly South Indian

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (with bone preferred)
  • 2 onions (sliced), 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Whole spices: cinnamon, cloves, fennel
  • Chettinad masala (roasted & ground dry red chilies, coriander seeds, peppercorns, cumin)
  • Curry leaves, oil, salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, add whole spices and curry leaves.
  2. Sauté onions till golden, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook till soft. Mix in Chettinad masala.
  4. Add chicken, salt, and cook until tender and coated in masala.
  5. Serve hot with rice, parotta, or dosa!
