Meen Kulambu

Tangy, spicy, and swimming in tradition

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (seer/vanjaram or any firm fish)
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Tamarind extract 1/2 cup
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds
  • Chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, curry leaves
  • Oil, salt, and fresh coriander

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, splutter mustard & fenugreek seeds, add curry leaves.
  2. Sauté onions, then tomatoes, and ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add all spices, mix well. Pour in tamarind extract + 1 cup water.
  4. Let it boil, then gently add fish pieces.
  5. Simmer until fish is cooked and gravy thickens. Serve with hot rice!
