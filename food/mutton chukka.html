Mutton Chukka

Spicy, dry-roasted mutton bursting with Chettinad magic!

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton (small pieces)
  • 1 onion (sliced), 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder
  • Curry leaves, oil, salt, coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook mutton with salt and turmeric till tender.
  2. Heat oil, sauté onions, add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add cooked mutton, dry spices, and curry leaves.
  4. Cook until the masala coats the meat and turns dry.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or parotta!
