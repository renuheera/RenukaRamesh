Appam with Coconut Milk

Soft, lacy appams paired with sweet, creamy coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice (soaked for 4-5 hours)
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • For coconut milk: fresh grated coconut, water, and jaggery (optional)

Instructions

  1. Grind soaked raw rice, cooked rice, and grated coconut into a smooth batter.
  2. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water, let it activate for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix yeast water with batter, add salt, and ferment overnight.
  4. Heat appam pan, pour a ladle of batter, swirl to make thin edges and thicker center.
  5. Cover and cook till edges are crispy and center is soft and fluffy.
  6. For coconut milk, blend fresh coconut with water and strain. Sweeten with jaggery if desired.
  7. Serve hot appams with fresh coconut milk for the perfect combo!
