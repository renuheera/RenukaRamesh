Ambur Biriyani

Fragrant, fiery, and fit for royalty

Ingredients

  • 2 cups seeraga samba rice
  • 500g chicken (or mutton)
  • 1 cup curd, 2 onions, 2 tomatoes
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, star anise)
  • Red chili paste (soaked red chilies ground to paste)
  • Mint, coriander, ginger garlic paste, ghee, salt

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with curd, red chili paste, and salt set aside.
  2. In ghee, sauté spices, onions, and ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes, mint, coriander cook till mushy.
  4. Add marinated chicken, cook till done. Add rice and water (1:1.5 ratio).
  5. Cook covered until rice is fluffy and aromatic. Serve with raita and brinjal gravy!
