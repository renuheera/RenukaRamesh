How to Bake a Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.
  5. Alternately add milk and dry ingredients, mixing gently.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool before removing it from the pan.
  9. Decorate with frosting or toppings as desired.
  10. Serve and enjoy your delicious cake!
