BEAGLE:Small Hounds, Big Hearts

Beagles are smart, loving, and high-energy dogs, renowned for their sharp olfactory ability and inquisitive nature. They are great family pets because they are gentle-natured, friendly, and socially inclined animals that live for companionship.

Beagles love active homes with constant exercise, mental challenges, and interaction, which makes them devoted and cheerful members of any family.
