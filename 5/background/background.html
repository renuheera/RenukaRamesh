Kailasanathar Temple in Kanchipuram is a 7th-century sandstone Shiva temple, built by Pallavas, famous for its Dravidian architecture, intricate carvings, and spiritual symbolism.
The Vijaya Vittala Temple in Hampi, Karnataka is a 15th-century marvel known for its musical pillars, stone chariot, and grand Dravidian architecture. A UNESCO heritage gem!
